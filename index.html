Sneak Attack Game

How to Play

  • Arrow Btns = move. To remain still, press the Camouflage Btn when it's your turn.
  • "Camouflage" makes the player the same color as the tile that the player is on.
  • "Glow an opponent" removes the camouflage, and opponent becomes visible.
  • Attack range = 2 Manhattan steps. If attack succeeds, you win! If attack fails, the opponent wins!
  • The other way to win is to be first to reach the opposite corner.(Opponent's starting tile). The colored 'doors' at the edges will funnel you back to the center of the grid.
